Agra

Agra is a city offering a discovery of the beautiful era. Agra has a rich
history, reflected in its numerous monuments dotted in and around the
city. The earliest citation for Agra comes from the mythological era,
where the epic Mahabharata refer Agra as 'Agravana' meaning paradise
in Sanskrit.
